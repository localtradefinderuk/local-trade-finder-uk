Why I built Local Trade Finder UK

I created Local Trade Finder UK to help customers find trusted local tradespeople without having to ask for recommendations all over social media — or picking someone at random from the internet.

The goal is simple: make it easier to find the right trade with confidence, using clear profiles and honest feedback.

  • Local-first directory of trades
  • Clear profiles with services and coverage
  • Ratings and reviews to build trust
  • Simple, clean and easy to use

For customers

Find the right person for the job with more confidence. Ratings and reviews help you choose trusted tradespeople in your area.

How to use the website

  1. Go to the Customer tab
  2. Select your service type, trade and region
  3. Enter your postcode and search
  4. Read profiles, check ratings, and contact the trader directly
